Achtelbass is meant to help you practice sight reading at your individual level.

All notes are randomly generated and very unlikely to be seen ever again in the same combination.

This way you will not memorize the notes of the exercise as it happens often with traditional literature.

You can increase the difficulty level gradually by adding options evenly or concentrate on certain options like triplets, chords, exotic time signatures or whatever you want to master.

Prepare for exams, target your personal weak spots or just begin to learn sight reading.

Achtelbass is free of charge and does not bother you with advertisement.

If you miss a feature or find a bug please contact me at соntаct@achtelbass.de or open an issue at github.

If you are into python then you might fork the project and fix the issue by yourself.

If you do not code but like the website and want to contribute you can make a donation.
