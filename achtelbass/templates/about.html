Achtelbass is meant to help you practice sight reading at your individual level.

Prepare for exams, target your personal weak spots or just begin to learn sight reading.

Achtelbass is free of charge, does not set cookies and will not bother you with advertisement.

If you miss a feature or find a bug please contact me at spam@achtelbass.de or open an issue at github.

If you know python and html then you might even fork the project and fix the issue by yourself.

If you do not code but like the website and want to contribute you can make a donation.
